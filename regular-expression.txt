Regular Expression
definition: a sequence of characters that defines a search pattern 
\ means the start 
/ means the end 
. means any character
\d means any digit
^.... starts
() used to capture a group 
[a-z] any character 
[0-9] numbers 0 to 9
(.*)  capture all 
\s spacebar or whitespace
* zero  or more repetitions 
+ one or more repetitions
^...$ starts and end 
...$ ends 
[^]is used to express not
